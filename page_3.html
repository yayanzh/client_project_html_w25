University Health & Counseling (UHC) supports the health and well-being of the campus community. We are inspired to help students realize their potential, cope with the stresses of life, work productively, and connect meaningfully with others.

UHC is made up of Counseling and Psychological Services (CAPS), which offers counseling services, and University Health Service (UHS), which offers medical services. For information about UHS, please visit the UHS website.

What does CAPS offer?

  • Brief, Solution-Focused Individual Counseling
  • Group Counseling
  • Couples Counseling
  • Drop-in Workshops
  • Consultations
  • Urgent/Crisis Services
  • Case Management and Referrals
  • Community Engagement & Outreach
